## Response Management

### Positive Responses
- **Immediate**: Send requested materials within 24 hours
- **Timeline**: Provide realistic delivery timelines
- **Quality**: Ensure content exceeds expectations
- **Follow-through**: Complete all commitments promptly

### Negative/No Responses
- **Week 1**: Send polite follow-up with additional value
- **Week 2**: Try different angle or contact person
- **Month 1**: Final follow-up with new resource/angle
- **Quarter 1**: Add to long-term nurture list

### Relationship Building
- **Social Media**: Follow and engage with successful contacts
- **Industry Events**: Note speaking/attending opportunities
- **News Monitoring**: Share relevant insights with new contacts
- **Content Updates**: Notify contacts of relevant new resources
