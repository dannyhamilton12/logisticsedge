## Engagement Protocol

- **Daily:** Comment on 5-10 relevant posts from logistics influencers, industry leaders, and target publication editors
- **Response time:** Reply to all comments on our posts within 4 hours during business hours
- **Link placement:** Always put article links in first comment, not in post body (LinkedIn algorithm penalises external links in post body)
- **UTM tagging:** All shared links must use UTM parameters: `?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=[post-topic]`
