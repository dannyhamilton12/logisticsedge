## Customization Guidelines

### Research Before Sending
1. Check recent articles/posts by the publication/organization
2. Identify specific editor/contact names where possible
3. Reference recent work or achievements to show familiarity
4. Align messaging with their content style and audience needs

### Personalization Elements
- Specific recent content they've published
- Relevant industry challenges they've covered
- Their audience demographics and interests
- Their content calendar or upcoming themes

### Success Metrics Per Template Type
- **Publications**: Open rate >25%, response rate >8%, conversion rate >3%
- **Associations**: Open rate >35%, response rate >15%, conversion rate >8%
- **Government**: Open rate >20%, response rate >5%, conversion rate >2%
- **Communities**: Engagement rate >10%, click-through rate >5%

Use these templates as starting points but always customize based on specific research about each target.
