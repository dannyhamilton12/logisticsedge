## Tracking & Metrics

### Key Metrics to Monitor
1. **Outreach Volume**: Emails sent, responses received, conversion rate
2. **Link Acquisition**: Total backlinks gained, referring domains
3. **Traffic Impact**: Referral traffic from acquired links
4. **Domain Authority**: Target site DA/DR scores
5. **Brand Mentions**: Unlinked mentions discovered and converted

### Tracking Tools Setup
- **Ahrefs/SEMrush**: Backlink monitoring and competitor analysis
- **Google Search Console**: Traffic and keyword performance
- **Google Analytics**: Referral traffic attribution
- **CRM Integration**: Outreach campaign management

### Monthly Review Process
1. Analyze new backlinks and their traffic contribution
2. Review outreach campaign performance and optimize templates
3. Identify new target opportunities based on competitor analysis
4. Update resource quality and relevance for ongoing campaigns

## Success Criteria

### 3-Month Goals
- 25 high-quality backlinks from industry-relevant sources
- 5 guest posting placements in industry publications
- 10 resource page inclusions on association/government sites
- 15% increase in organic traffic to target articles

### 6-Month Goals
- 50 high-quality backlinks with average DR 40+
- Partnership agreements with 3 major trade associations
- Regular contributor status at 2 industry publications
- 30% increase in brand searches and direct traffic

This strategy focuses on building authoritative, relevant backlinks that will improve our search rankings while establishing LogisticsEdge as the go-to resource for UK trade compliance guidance.
