## Pitch Deployment Notes

- Send **one pitch per publication** — do not pitch the same topic to competing publications simultaneously
- If the primary target declines, wait 48 hours then pitch to the secondary
- Personalise the opening line to reference a recent article from the publication
- Follow up once at 7 days if no response; move to next target at 14 days
- Negotiate author bio with do-follow link to logisticsedge.co.uk
- Never submit identical content to LogisticsEdge articles — always rewrite for the publication's audience and angle
