# Link-Building SOPs — Repeatable Outreach Process

**Last updated:** 4 April 2026 | **Owner:** Growth Manager

## SOP 1: Guest Post Outreach

### Purpose
Secure editorial backlinks from trade publications through contributed articles.

### Process

1. **Identify target publication**
   - Check the link targets list (`01-link-targets-and-outreach.md`) for Tier 1 and Tier 2 publications
   - Verify they accept contributed content (check "contribute" or "write for us" pages)
   - Review 3-5 recent articles to understand their editorial style and audience

2. **Prepare the pitch**
   - Select a topic from the guest post pitches (`02-guest-post-pitches.md`) or develop a new one
   - Personalise the opening to reference a recent article from the publication
   - Ensure the topic hasn't been covered by them in the last 6 months

3. **Send the pitch**
   - Use Template A from the outreach templates
   - Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9:00-11:00 AM UK time (highest open rates)
   - Subject line must include the specific topic and publication name

4. **Follow-up cadence**
   - Day 7: First follow-up — brief, polite, reference original email
   - Day 14: Second follow-up — offer alternative topic angle
   - Day 14+: No further follow-ups. Mark as "no response" and move to next target

5. **On acceptance**
   - Confirm word count, deadline, and editorial guidelines
   - Write original content (never duplicate LogisticsEdge articles — rewrite for their audience)
   - Include 1-2 natural links to LogisticsEdge articles within the body
   - Negotiate author bio with do-follow link to logisticsedge.co.uk
   - Submit on time. Follow up within 24 hours of submission to confirm receipt

6. **On publication**
   - Verify the backlink is live and do-follow
   - Share the published piece on all social channels
   - Add to the outreach tracker with link URL and publication date
   - Send a thank-you email to the editor — maintain the relationship for future pitches

### Time investment
- Pitch preparation: 15 minutes per target
- Article writing: 2-3 hours per piece
- Total per successful guest post: ~4 hours

## SOP 2: Resource Page Link Requests

### Purpose
Get LogisticsEdge guides listed on relevant resource pages of trade associations, industry bodies, and educational institutions.

### Process

1. **Find resource pages**
   - Check link targets list for Tier 2 and Tier 3 targets
   - Google: `site:[target-domain] "resources" OR "useful links" OR "recommended reading"`
   - Look for pages listing external guides, tools, or educational content

2. **Verify relevance**
   - The resource page must serve an audience that would benefit from our content
   - Our content must be substantially better or different from what's already listed
   - The page must have been updated within the last 12 months (check Wayback Machine if needed)

3. **Send the request**
   - Use Template B from the outreach templates
   - Reference the specific resource page URL (not just the site homepage)
   - Explain why the content is useful for their specific audience
   - Be specific about which LogisticsEdge guide fits

4. **Follow-up**
   - Day 7: One follow-up only
   - No further chasing — resource page owners either add you or they don't

5. **Track results**
   - Update the outreach tracker with response and link status
   - If added, verify the link quarterly (resource pages get reorganised)

### Time investment
- 10 minutes per request (including research)
- Expected conversion rate: 5-15%

## SOP 3: Directory Submissions

### Purpose
Build foundational backlinks from logistics and business directories.

### Process

1. **Identify directories**
   - Check link targets list for Tier 3 directory targets
   - Search for: `"UK logistics directory" OR "supply chain directory" submit`
   - Focus on niche directories (logistics-specific) over general business directories

2. **Prepare listing**
   - Company name: LogisticsEdge
   - URL: logisticsedge.co.uk
   - Category: Logistics / Supply Chain / Customs Compliance
   - Description: Use the 150-character site description, adjusted for each directory
   - Always include the primary URL and 1-2 article URLs where allowed

3. **Submit**
   - Use Template D if email submission required
   - For self-service directories, complete the form directly
   - Screenshot the submission for records

4. **Verify**
   - Check listing is live within 2 weeks
   - Verify link is correct and clickable
   - Flag any nofollow links (not worth chasing for corrections, but note for prioritisation)

### Time investment
- 5-10 minutes per directory
- One-time effort, verify quarterly

## SOP 4: Cross-Promotion Partnerships

### Purpose
Exchange backlinks with complementary (non-competing) sites in the logistics and customs space.

### Process

1. **Identify partners**
   - Sites that cover adjacent topics (customs brokerage, freight forwarding, trade finance)
   - Must not directly compete with LogisticsEdge content topics
   - Should have similar or higher domain authority

2. **Evaluate fit**
   - Their content must be genuinely useful to our audience
   - They must have content we can naturally reference in our articles
   - Avoid link farms, PBNs, or sites with spammy backlink profiles

3. **Propose the exchange**
   - Use Template C from the outreach templates
   - Be specific about which of their pages we'd link to and where
   - Offer a specific LogisticsEdge page for them to link to
   - Frame as mutual audience value, not link exchange

4. **Implement**
   - Add their link naturally within a relevant article (not a dedicated "partners" page)
   - Verify they add our link in a similarly natural placement
   - Both links should be contextual and do-follow

5. **Maintain**
   - Check partner links quarterly
   - If they remove our link, send a polite enquiry before removing theirs

### Time investment
- 20 minutes per partnership (outreach + implementation)
- Ongoing: 5 minutes per quarter for verification

## SOP 5: Journalist Query Responses

### Purpose
Earn editorial mentions and backlinks by responding to journalist queries.

### Process

1. **Monitor sources**
   - Source of Sources (SOS): Check 3x daily emails
   - Qwoted: Check profile matches weekly
   - #JournoRequest on X/Twitter: Set up alerts
   - ResponseSource: Subscribe from Month 2 (£20/month)

2. **Filter queries**
   - Only respond to queries related to: logistics, customs, supply chain, import/export, CBAM, trade compliance
   - Skip queries that require sharing confidential company data
   - Skip queries from publications with DA below 20

3. **Respond fast**
   - Must respond within 2 hours of query publication (speed wins placements)
   - Use Template C from the outreach templates as starting point
   - Lead with the most quotable insight — not credentials
   - Keep response under 200 words unless they request more
   - Always include: "[Name], founder of LogisticsEdge (logisticsedge.co.uk)"

4. **Follow up**
   - One follow-up at 48 hours if no response
   - After publication, share on social media and tag the journalist

### Time investment
- Daily monitoring: 10 minutes
- Per response: 15-20 minutes
- Expected placement rate: 10-20% of responses

## Outreach Tracker Template

Maintain a spreadsheet (or database) with these columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Target Site | Domain name |
| Target URL | Specific page URL |
| DA Estimate | Domain authority |
| Outreach Type | Guest post / Resource page / Directory / Partnership / Journalist |
| Contact Name | Editor or webmaster name |
| Contact Email | Email address |
| First Contact Date | Date of initial outreach |
| Follow-up 1 Date | First follow-up date |
| Follow-up 2 Date | Second follow-up date |
| Response | Yes / No / Pending |
| Response Detail | What they said |
| Link Acquired | Yes / No |
| Link URL | URL where our link appears |
| Link Type | Do-follow / No-follow |
| Referring Page DA | Authority of the specific linking page |
| Date Acquired | When the link went live |
| Last Verified | Date last checked link is still live |
| Notes | Any relevant context |

## Monthly Review Process

On the last Friday of each month:

1. **Count links acquired** — new do-follow links gained this month
2. **Check existing links** — verify top 10 links are still live
3. **Review response rates** — which templates and approaches are converting
4. **Update targets** — add new targets, remove unresponsive ones
5. **Adjust templates** — refine based on what's getting responses
6. **Report metrics:**
   - Total backlinks acquired (cumulative)
   - New referring domains this month
   - Guest posts published
   - Journalist placements earned
   - Outreach response rate
   - Time spent vs links gained (efficiency)
