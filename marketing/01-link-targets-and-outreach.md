## Outreach Email Templates

### Template A — Guest Post Pitch (Trade Publications)

**Use for:** Targets 1-8, 14

```
Subject: Contributed article idea — [TOPIC] for [PUBLICATION NAME]

Hi [EDITOR NAME],

I run LogisticsEdge (logisticsedge.co.uk), a UK-focused resource for logistics
and supply chain professionals. I've been following your coverage of
[RECENT RELEVANT TOPIC THEY COVERED].

I'd like to contribute an article on [SPECIFIC TOPIC], covering:

- [KEY POINT 1 — practical, actionable]
- [KEY POINT 2 — data or research backed]
- [KEY POINT 3 — forward-looking]

I manage logistics operations for a major industrial OEM, so the content is
practical and based on direct experience — not theoretical.

The piece would be 800-1,200 words, original, and exclusive to
[PUBLICATION NAME]. Happy to adjust the angle to fit your editorial calendar.

Would this be of interest?

Best regards,
[NAME]
LogisticsEdge | logisticsedge.co.uk
```

### Template B — Resource Page Link Request

**Use for:** Targets 9-13, 19-20

```
Subject: Resource for your [SPECIFIC PAGE/SECTION]

Hi [NAME],

I came across your [SPECIFIC RESOURCE PAGE URL] — excellent collection
for [AUDIENCE].

We recently published a comprehensive guide on [TOPIC] that covers
[BRIEF VALUE SUMMARY — e.g., "the step-by-step EORI application process,
GB vs XI differences, and common mistakes causing customs delays"].

Link: [URL]

If you think it would be useful for your readers or members, I'd appreciate
inclusion on your resources page.

Best,
[NAME]
LogisticsEdge | logisticsedge.co.uk
```
